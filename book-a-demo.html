The demo is meant to answer operational questions, not create more of them. These are the topics teams usually want covered before they put time on the calendar.

What does the demo include?

You’ll get a personalized walkthrough of how Foster supports MDS review, from missed PDPM drivers and diagnosis support to evidence-backed follow-up. We focus on real workflow pressure points instead of a generic feature tour.

How quickly can Foster be deployed?

Deployment scope depends on your building and workflows, but the call is designed to show what rollout could realistically look like for your team before any commitment is made.

Does Foster work with PointClickCare-based workflows?

Yes. Foster is built for the documentation and review environment skilled nursing teams already work in, including the chart evidence and workflow patterns common to PointClickCare-centered operations.

Does Foster replace the MDS coordinator?

No. Foster is designed to strengthen coordinator judgment by bringing relevant documentation, missed opportunities, and follow-up actions into view faster.

Can your team show the evidence behind each recommendation?

Yes. Evidence-backed recommendations are core to the workflow, so the review process is faster to validate internally and easier to defend externally.
